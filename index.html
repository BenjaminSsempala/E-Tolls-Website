WHY E-TOLLS

In the New Vision newspaper of 8th January, 2022; an article was published discussing how the new tolling system of the Entebbe Express Highway had been finally implemented and generating returns of six hundred million shillings in just the first week. One problem that was however highlighted was the rise of unnecessary cues of vehicle at the tolling points, as payment of the toll currently requires interacting with a toller paid employed by Uganda National Roads Authority, as well as scanning a motorist having to scan their Epesa Card (a card which motorists can scan to gain access to the road. Such an inconvenience can lead to delays and time wastage thus the need to create a way for payments to be processed online.

WHAT WE DO

E-Tolls allows motorists to clear their toll fees from their mobile phones using their mobile money accounts or debit or credit cards. This application is supported on mobile phones on both Android and iOS operating systems.
